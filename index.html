5. Our Colorfull Attire by President
Meaning of attire could be defined as mode of dressing of one or a particular tribe. It also interprets one’s nationality (culture). For instance Hausa, Fulani, Igbo, Efik, Ibibio etc. attire are very educative, in olden days where there was nothing like western education, one fore- fathers used attire to identify where one comes from without using devices. At that primitive period of time, people had fear of committing crime of any kind order not to tarnish image of their culture. Then everybody or tribe see moral culture very important. In our nation today, people commit office of killing here and there because no more culture in the society, no more fear of god, no more teachings of basic moral values to our children. What we all talk about nowadays is modern world, modern technology, computer age etc. which can’t lead us to peaceful world and using all these measures to fight corruption are not earning solution; only going back and maintain our culture, corruption will fight itself. At these juncture, be inspired to show case to the world our identity and dignity. This colorful attire describes our union logo. In the logo, you will find some physical features that always remind us our culture E.G dressing, language, dancing, nature of corruption, and method of cooking and so on and so forth. Our attire here shows meekness, loyalty, greatness, the attire also signify always maintain peace and order, justice and equity in anywhere. The attire made us outstanding in public, one can’t commit crime and hide or go free with the attire, the whole space will be pointing accuse fingers at Efik and Ibibio. Yes because that’s our general mode of dressing which commands respect. In final note, I charge all our leaders everywhere to embrace and admire this our cultural attire. It is our identity and dignity that serve us all good Congratulations!!! God bless Nka Ima Ndito Uruan Hon. Raphael Bassey.
